Cruiser Tetron

Strengths Weaknesses
  • Extremely high damage output with almost all of his moves dealing more than 10 damage, and a few of them doing 20 or more damage.
  • A bunch of his moves have superarmor where they effectively act as counters, making him very good at predicting and punishing predictable attacks.
  • Flies at 18 speed using his U-special. This gives him a multitude of movement options in neutral, and an incredibly far horizontal recovery. A lot of the time his opponent simply won't have the opportunity to edgeguard him because he will almost always enough fuel to fly under the stage to safety.
  • The heaviest character in the game by a longshot, making it so he suffers the least hitstun out of any character and lives the longest both horizontally and vertically(?).
  • Very strong punish and techchase game. His D-air leads into his other aerials and his N-air pops them to the ground to be techchased.
  • One of the best grab games. Tetron possesses the strongest F-throw in the game, a weaker but still strong B-throw, and a U-throw that deals lots of damage and combos nonfloaties well.
  • Has a ton of knockback on most of his moves so he will rarely struggle to take the stock.
  • Has a projectile that has notably startup, but is uninteractible, fast and combos well.
  • Tetron is the tallest character while still being quite wide, making him a massive target for combos.
  • Although his superarmor is great for closing the gap and punishing attacks, he still has trouble with zoning and projectiles.
  • Even though he suffers the least hitstun, he is still combo good on behalf of him having a massive hurtbox and the heaviest weight, along with higher than average gravity.
  • Very limited vertical recovery. Though it is somewhat rare, he can die even earlier than most characters if he's hit by even a moderately strong spike without a jump.
  • Has a hard time dealing with ledgetraps.
